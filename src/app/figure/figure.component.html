The App

Why use it?

Take photos and learn about the surrounding landmarks. We have 2 opportunities to use our app. You can choose from different plans for your adventure trip. We offer you to pick out one city and start discovering it. We have 2 types of plans. The Free Plan does not give you the guide for the city and does not reveal the possible places to visit. If you can find and capture the sight only then you will receive information for the place and unlock it. The Premium Plan unlocks the guide feature which based on machine learning creates your personal path through the city and shows you all of the possible places. You can organize your trip alone because you will see the full path.
